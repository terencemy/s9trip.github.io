About My Website Journey

From a Malaysia Registered Tourist Guide to a long-term digital base

From Real Journeys to a Digital Base

I never planned to build a website.

For more than 20 years, my work has been grounded in real journeys, real people, and real-time decisions as a Malaysia Registered Tourist Guide. I am more familiar with solving problems on the road than working behind a screen.

However, travel has evolved. Many important decisions now happen before travellers even arrive, while valuable real-world experience often disappears within fast-moving platforms.

This website is my response to that change — a long-term digital base to document genuine travel experience, share practical insights, and preserve knowledge beyond algorithms and trends.

While I do provide online live chat support and work remotely at times, my core identity remains unchanged: I am still a local Malaysia Registered Tourist Guide and an outbound tour leader.

Technology is only a tool. Real experience is the foundation.

— Terence
Malaysia Registered Tourist Guide
