KLIA to KLIA2 Airport Free Shuttle Bus Guide

Official free shuttle service between Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 at Kuala Lumpur International Airport.

Cost

The shuttle bus service is provided free of charge. No reservation is required.

Operating Hours & Frequency

• Operates 24 hours daily
• Frequency: every 10–15 minutes

Travel Time

• KLIA T1 → T2: approx. 15 minutes (direct)
• KLIA T2 → T1: approx. 20–25 minutes (includes LTCP stop)

Onboard Experience

• Purple / pink shuttle bus
• LED route display (001 KLIA1 / LTCP)
• Seating for approx. 25 passengers + standing area
• Passengers manage their own luggage

Boarding Points

KLIA T1 → T2
Level 1, Door 4
Bus stop located approx. 10 metres outside
Look for “Free Shuttle Service” signage

KLIA T2 → T1
Level 1, Transport Hub
Exit 3 → Bay A10

Terminal Differences

KLIA T1: Full-service airlines; majority of international and domestic flights.
KLIA T2: Mainly budget airlines; international and domestic routes.
