Changi Airport to Johor Bahru by Cross-Border Coach

A calm, seamless journey on the Transtar TS1 service — curated by S9Trip.

Service Overview

The Transtar TS1 is a direct cross-border coach from Changi Airport to Larkin Terminal, Johor Bahru. It is ideal for travellers who prefer a simple and stress-free journey without multiple transfers.

Boarding Information

  • Terminal 1: Arrival Coach Bay B / C
  • Terminal 4: Arrival Coach Bay 7 / 8

Travellers arriving at Terminal 2 or 3 should proceed to Terminal 1 via Skytrain or walking route.

Route & Immigration

The coach travels via Woodlands Checkpoint. Immigration is completed together with the bus, and passengers continue on the same vehicle throughout the process.

Fares & Payment

  • Changi Airport → Larkin Terminal: Adult S$11 | Child S$5.50
  • Larkin Terminal → Changi Airport: Adult RM13 | Child RM6.50

Payment is by cash or EZ-Link only. Tickets are non-refundable and exact payment is recommended.
